Scorekeepers

The following are the list of available reports based on data collected for scorekeepers.

  • Appearance Counts by Year

    A collection of scorekeeper appearance counts for regular, Best Of, repeat Best Ofs, repeat and all shows grouped by year.

    Regular show counts do not include Best Of or repeat shows. Counts for both Best Of and repeat shows do not include repeat Best Of shows. Counts for all shows should be the sum of regular, Best Of, repeat and repeat Best Of show counts.

  • Appearance Counts by Year: Grid

    A large table displaying each scorekeeper as rows and appearance counts for each year as columns. Scorekeeper appearance counts includes regular shows and all shows, with the count of all shows in parentheses.

    Regular shows do not include Best Of or repeat shows, while all shows include all available shows.

  • Appearance Summary

    A list of scorekeepers with their first and most recent appearances and corresponding count of appearances for both regular shows and all shows.

    Regular shows do not include Best Of or repeat shows, while all shows include all available shows.

  • Appearances by Year

    A collection of appearances with show information, including location, host, panelists and Not My Job guest(s), for a selected scorekeeper grouped by year.

  • Debuts by Year

    A collection of scorekeeper debut appearances with show information, including location, host, panelists and Not My Job guest(s), grouped by year.

  • Scorekeeper Introductions

    A collection of scorekeepers with a list of introductions that they have used at the start of each show they appeared on.
