Guests

The following are the list of available reports based on data collected for Not My Job guests.

  • Appearances by Year

    This report provides a list of Not My Job guests appear for a given year.

    Guest appearances include appearances on regular shows and unique appearances only on Best Of shows.

  • Best Of Only Not My Job Guests

    This report provides a list of Not My Job guests that have only appeared on Best Of shows and not on any regular show. These guests would have made their appearances on second live tapings when Wait Wait Don't Tell Me! travel to other cities.

  • Most Appearances

    This report provides a list of Not My Job guests and the number of appearances that they have made across all shows and regular shows. The list is ordered based on the number of appearances for all shows in descending order.

  • Not My Job Guests with Missing Scores

    This report provides a list of Not My Job guests that do not have scores entered for any given reason.

    The goal for this report is highlight potentially missing data that will need to be tracked down and for this report to eventually return no data.

  • Not My Job Scoring Exceptions

    This report provides a list of Not My Job guests that have a scoring exception that allowed them to win the prize for the listener contestant, even though they didn't score 2 or more points. An exception is also noted if the guest was awarded a win or all 3 points no matter their actual score.

  • Not My Job Three Pointers

    This report provides a list of all of the Not My Job guests who answered all three Not My Job questions correctly or were awarded all three points via a scoring exception.

  • Wins by Year

    This report provides a list of Not My Job guests who scored a win by scoring 2 or more points or via a scoring exception for a given year.

    Guest appearances include appearances on regular shows and unique appearances only on Best Of shows.

    Note: While Susan Stamberg received a scoring exception on the 1998-05-02 show, but Susan was not granted a win by Carl Kasell.
