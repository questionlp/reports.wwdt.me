Hosts

The following are the list of available reports based on data collected for show hosts.

  • Appearance Counts by Year

    A collection of show host appearance counts for regular, Best Of, repeat Best Ofs, repeat and all shows grouped by year.

    Regular show counts do not include Best Of or repeat shows. Counts for both Best Of and repeat shows do not include repeat Best Of shows. Counts for all shows should be the sum of regular, Best Of, repeat and repeat Best Of show counts.

  • Appearance Counts by Year: Grid

    A large table displaying each show host as rows and hosting appearance counts for each year as columns. Hosting appearance counts includes regular shows and all shows, with the count of all shows in parentheses.

    Regular shows do not include Best Of or repeat shows, while all shows include all available shows.

  • Appearance Summary

    A list of show hosts with their first and most recent appearances and corresponding count of hosting appearances for both regular shows and all shows.

    Regular shows do not include Best Of or repeat shows, while all shows include all available shows.

  • Appearances by Year

    A collection of appearances with show information, including location, scorekeeper, panelists and Not My Job guest(s), for a selected show host grouped by year.

  • Debuts by Year

    A collection of show host debut appearances with show information, including location, scorekeeper, panelists and Not My Job guest(s), grouped by year.
