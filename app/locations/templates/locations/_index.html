Locations

The following are the list of available reports based on data collected for show locations.

  • Average Scores by Location

    A list of recording locations with venue name, city, state, average panelist score and average panelist total score, and a count shows recorded at the location. The locations are sorted in descending order based on average scores.

    Panelist scores from the 20th anniversary special show, that aired on October 27, 2018, are omitted due to the unique Lightning Fill-in-the-Blank format used for that show.

  • Home vs Away

    A list of the number of shows recorded at home (any venue or studio located in Chicago, Illinois), away, and at home/remote studios, grouped by year.

  • Recording Counts by Year

    A collection of show location recording counts for regular, Best Of, repeat Best Ofs, repeat and all shows grouped by year.

    Regular show counts do not include Best Of or repeat shows. Counts for both Best Of and repeat shows do not include repeat Best Of shows. Counts for all shows should be the sum of regular, Best Of, repeat and repeat Best Of show counts.

  • Recordings by Year

    A list of recordings including host, scorekeeper, panelists and Not My Job guest, for a selected location grouped by year.
