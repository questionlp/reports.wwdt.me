Locations

The following are the list of available reports based on data collected for show locations.

  • Average Scores by Location

    This report provides a list of show locations and the corresponding average panelist score and average total score, in descending order.

    One exception from the scoring is the 25th Anniversary Special, which aired on October 27, 2018. The show's total score has been excluded due to the unique Lightning Fill-in-the-Blank format.

  • Home vs Away

    This report provides a count of shows recorded at home (any venue or studio located in Chicago, Illinois), away and home/remote studios, broken down by year.

  • Recordings by Year

    This report provides a list of locations where shows have recorded for a given year.

    Guest appearances include appearances on regular shows and unique appearances only on Best Of shows.
